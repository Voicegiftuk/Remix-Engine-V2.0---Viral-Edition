# PRODUCT BACKGROUND IMAGES

Place your product photography here for image mockup generation.

## Requirements

- **Format**: .jpg or .png
- **Resolution**: 1080x1080 minimum (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait)
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, professional
- **Quantity**: Minimum 5 images, recommended 20+ for variety

## What to Photograph

Products that work well with NFC stickers:

### Physical Products
- Wine bottles (various angles)
- Greeting cards (blank or designed)
- Gift boxes (wrapped, different sizes)
- Photo frames (with photos)
- Journals/notebooks
- Books
- Coffee mugs
- Gift bags

### Surfaces
- Clean wooden table
- White desk surface
- Gray modern surface
- Textured backgrounds

### Occasions
- Birthday setup (balloons, decorations)
- Wedding items (invitations, favors)
- Anniversary (flowers, candles)
- Corporate (business cards, folders)

## Photography Tips

### Lighting
- Natural window light is best
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Even, soft lighting
- No glare on reflective surfaces

### Composition
- Center product in frame
- Clean, minimal background
- Slight angle (not flat overhead)
- Room for sticker placement

### Quality
- Sharp focus on product
- Proper exposure (not too dark/bright)
- Color accuracy
- No clutter in background

## Image Preparation

### Recommended Sizes
- Square: 1080x1080 (Instagram feed)
- Portrait: 1080x1350 (Instagram feed tall)
- Story: 1080x1920 (full vertical)

### Editing Tips
- Crop to remove distractions
- Adjust brightness/contrast
- Enhance colors slightly
- Keep natural look
