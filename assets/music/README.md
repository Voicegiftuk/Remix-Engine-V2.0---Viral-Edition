# BACKGROUND MUSIC

Place your royalty-free background music here for video generation.

## Requirements

- **Format**: .mp3 or .wav
- **Duration**: 15-60 seconds (will loop if needed)
- **Quality**: 128kbps minimum, 320kbps recommended
- **Rights**: Royalty-free or properly licensed
- **Quantity**: Minimum 5 tracks, recommended 10+ for variety

## Music Style Recommendations

### Upbeat/Happy
- For birthday, celebration videos
- 120-140 BPM
- Major key, positive feel

### Emotional/Sentimental
- For anniversary, love messages
- 80-100 BPM
- Soft, touching melodies

### Corporate/Professional
- For business thank you messages
- 100-120 BPM
- Clean, modern sound

### Ambient/Background
- General purpose
- 90-110 BPM
- Non-intrusive, subtle

## Free Royalty-Free Music Sources

### YouTube Audio Library
- URL: studio.youtube.com/channel/UC/music
- License: Free for commercial use
- Filter: "No attribution required"

### Bensound
- URL: bensound.com
- License: Free with attribution (or purchase license)
- High quality, professional tracks

### Incompetech
- URL: incompetech.com/music
- License: Free with attribution (or purchase license)
- Extensive library

### Pixabay Music
- URL: pixabay.com/music
- License: Free for commercial use, no attribution
- Growing library

### Free Music Archive
- URL: freemusicarchive.org
- License: Various (check each track)
- Independent artists

## Music Selection Tips

### Avoid
- ❌ Copyrighted commercial music
- ❌ Music with lyrics (distracting)
- ❌ Very loud/aggressive music
- ❌ Music that changes tempo dramatically
- ❌ Low quality recordings

### Prefer
- ✅ Instrumental only
- ✅ Consistent energy level
- ✅ Clear, professional production
- ✅ Fits brand mood (warm, modern)
- ✅ Loopable (smooth ending)

## Naming Convention

```
music_upbeat_01.mp3
music_emotional_02.mp3
music_corporate_03.mp3
music_ambient_04.mp3
...
```

## Volume Levels

Music will be automatically set to 30% volume (0.3) to ensure:
- Voice/message is primary
- Music is background only
- Professional audio mix

## Attribution

If using tracks requiring attribution, keep a file:

```
MUSIC_CREDITS.txt
---
Track: "Sunny Day"
Artist: Artist Name
Source: Bensound.com
License: Free with attribution
```

## Quick Start

1. Visit pixabay.com/music
2. Search: "upbeat instrumental", "background music"
3. Download 5-10 tracks (different moods)
4. Rename clearly (music_upbeat_01.mp3, etc.)
5. Add to this folder
6. Done!
