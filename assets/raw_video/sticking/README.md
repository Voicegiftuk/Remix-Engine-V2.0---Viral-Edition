# STICKING VIDEO CLIPS

Place your "hand placing sticker" video clips here.

## Requirements

- **Format**: .mp4 or .mov
- **Duration**: 3-5 seconds per clip
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum (1920x1080 or higher)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical/portrait)
- **Quantity**: Minimum 10 clips, recommended 20+ for more variety

## What to Film

Record hand placing NFC sticker on various products:

1. Wine bottle
2. Greeting card
3. Gift box
4. Photo frame
5. Journal/notebook
6. Door/wall
7. Laptop
8. Mug/cup
9. Book cover
10. Gift bag

## Filming Tips

- **Lighting**: Bright, even lighting (avoid shadows)
- **Focus**: Clear view of sticker and product
- **Movement**: Smooth, deliberate motion
- **Angle**: 45Â° from above
- **Background**: Clean, uncluttered
- **Stability**: Use tripod or stable surface
