# SCANNING VIDEO CLIPS

Place your "phone scanning NFC sticker" video clips here.

## Requirements

- **Format**: .mp4 or .mov
- **Duration**: 2-3 seconds per clip
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical/portrait)
- **Quantity**: Minimum 10 clips, recommended 20+ for variety

## What to Film

Record hand with phone approaching and tapping NFC sticker:

1. Top approach scan
2. Side approach scan
3. Close-up tap
4. Left hand scanning
5. Right hand scanning
6. Different phone model #1
7. Different phone model #2
8. Vertical surface scan (door/wall)
9. Horizontal surface scan (table)
10. Slow motion tap

## Filming Tips

- **Phone Visibility**: Show phone back/screen when possible
- **Movement**: Natural, confident tapping motion
- **Contact Point**: Pause slightly at tap (0.5 sec)
- **Angle**: Profile view showing both phone and sticker
- **Clarity**: Focus on both elements clearly
