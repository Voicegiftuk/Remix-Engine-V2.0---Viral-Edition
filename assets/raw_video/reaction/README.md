# REACTION VIDEO CLIPS

Place your "phone screen showing message playback" video clips here.

## Requirements

- **Format**: .mp4 or .mov
- **Duration**: 3-5 seconds per clip
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical/portrait)
- **Quantity**: Minimum 10 clips, recommended 20+ for variety

## What to Record

Screen recordings of video messages playing:

1. Birthday message ("Happy Birthday!")
2. Anniversary message ("Happy Anniversary honey!")
3. Thank you message
4. Love declaration ("I love you...")
5. Congratulations message
6. Miss you message
7. Baby announcement
8. Wedding vows excerpt
9. Memorial tribute
10. Corporate thank you

## Recording Methods

### Method 1: Screen Recording
- iOS: Settings → Control Center → Screen Recording
- Android: Quick Settings → Screen Recorder
- Record your phone playing a video message

### Method 2: Use Existing Videos
- Download royalty-free emotional clips
- Use text-to-speech for messages
- Record friends/family (with permission)

### Method 3: SayPlay Demo Videos
- Use existing SayPlay demonstration videos
- Screen record the playback interface

## Content Tips

- **Audio Quality**: Clear, emotional delivery
- **Message Length**: 3-5 seconds of actual message
- **Variety**: Mix happy, emotional, professional tones
- **Interface**: Show play button initially
- **Clean Screen**: No notifications, full brightness
